Albert Einstein

Was he actually a little green man?
Albert Einstein - General Theory of Relativity, Nobel prize winner.

    Einsteins life:

  • 1879 - Born March 14th, Ulm, Germany.
  • 1880 - Einstein's move to Munich.
  • 1881 - Sister Maja (Maria) is born.
  • 1884 - Compass -his father shows him a pocket compass and is astounded by the thought that something unseen was moving the needle.
  • 1885-1888 - Attends a Catholic school, the family do not observe Jewish religious practices.
  • 1889 - Attends Luitpold Gymnasium in Munich for the next six years. Max Talmud a family friend introduces young Einstein to books on Science and mathematics. He starts his quest for facts and information available on the sciences.
  • 1894 - The family move to Pavia, Italy for finacial reasons. Albert stays in Munich to complete his studies but soon follows the family to Italy.
  • 1895 - Sent to Aarau, Switzerland to finisg school.
  • 1896 - Graduates from school at the age of 17 and enrolls at the ETH (Federal Polytechnic) in Zurich. Renounces his German citizenship.
  • 1898 - Meets and falls in love with Mileva Maric, a Hungarian classmate at the ETH.
  • 1900 - Graduates in 1900 from ETH with a degree in physics.
  • 1901 - Acquires Swiss citizenship. He completes his first scientific paper on the capillary forces of a straw. Mileva becomes pregnant and moves to live with her parents in to Hungary to give birth to their baby. Albert moves to Bern.
  • 1902 - Daughter called Lieserl Einstein who is later given up for adoption. Gets a job at the Swiss Patent Office.
  • 1903 - Einstein marries Mileva, January 6th.
  • 1904 - Albert and Mileva's first son, Hans Albert Einstein, was born in Berne, Switzerland, May 14th, 1904.
  • 1905 - Special Theory of Relativity is born and he applies his theory to mass and energy and formulates the famous equation e=mc2. Einstein has four papers published in the Annalen der Physik, the leading German physics journal. 1905 is known as Einstein's "Wonderful Year".
  • 1907 - Applies the laws of gravity to his Special Theory of Relativity.
  • 1908 - Becomes a privatdozent (an associate professorship) at the University of Bern.
  • 1909 - Resigns from Patent Office and is appointed Associate Professor of theoretical physics at Zurich University
  • 1910 - Albert and Mileva's second son, Eduard, was born in Munich, July 28th, 1910. He writes a paper on critical opalescence that described why the sky is blue.
  • 1911 - Predicts bending of light. He is appointed Professor of theoretical physics at the German University of Prague.
  • 1913 - Appointed Professor of theoretical physics at the Federal Institute of Technology, Zurich and works on his new Theory of Gravity.
  • 1914 - Albert and Mileva separate and live apart. Einstein is appointed Professor at University of Berlin, WW1 begins and Einstein signs anti-war "Manifesto to Europeans".
  • 1915 - Completes the General Theory of Relativity. Joins the "New Fatherland League" a pacifist organization.
  • 1916 - General Theory of Relativity is published.
  • 1917 - Writes his first paper on cosmology. He is Appointed Director of Kaiser Wilhelm Institute for Physics in Berlin.
  • 1918 - Falls seriously ill and is nursed by his cousin, Elsa Einstein Loewenthal.
  • 1919 - Einstein and Mileva divorce and Einstein marries his cousin Elsa on May 29. A solar eclipse proves Einstein's General Theory of Relativity
  • 1921 - Visits the U.S. and lectures at Princeton University on theory of relativity
  • 1922 - Awarded the Nobel Prize in physics for 1921
  • 1923 - Visits the Far East and Spain, lecturing at various universities.
  • 1924 - The "Einstein-Institute" in Potsdam begins its activities.
  • 1925 - Visits various countries in South America. He joins the Board of Governors and Academic Council of The Hebrew University.
  • 1927 - Begins to develop the foundation of quantum mechanics with Niels Bohr.
  • 1928 - Purses his idea of a unified field theory. He becomes ill and enlargement of the heart is diagnosed.
  • 1930-1933 - Einstein makes several visits to the USA delivering lectures at universities.
  • 1933 - Adolf Hitler appointed Chancellor of Germany. Albert Einstein leaves Germany and emigrates to U.S. in September and settles in Princeton, New Jersey where he assumes a post at the Institute for Advanced Study.
  • 1934 - The World As I See It published.
  • 1935 - The Einstein-Podolsky-Rosen paradox is published.
  • 1936 - Elsa Einstein dies.
  • 1939 - World War 2 starts and Albert Einstein warns President Roosevelt that Germany might build an atomic bomb. He then recommends nuclear research.
  • 1940 - Becomes a citizen of the United States but also retains his Swiss citizenship.
  • 1943 - Works with the Research and Development Division of the U.S. Navy on Ammunition and Explosives.
  • 1945 - World War 2 ends with the terrible nuclear bombing of Hiroshima and Nagasaki.
  • 1946 - Appointed chairman of the Emergency Committee for Atomic Scientists.
  • 1947 - Works on behalf of the cause for disarmament.
  • 1949 - Mileva dies.
  • 1952 - Offered presidency of the State of Israel.
  • 1955 - Co-signs the Russell-Einstein Manifesto warning of the nuclear threat. April 18th, Einstein dies from a ruptured aneurysm.
